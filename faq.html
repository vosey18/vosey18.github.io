Why a chatbot?

“Chatbots are the new apps” - Microsoft’s CEO Satya Nadella. We truly believe this. Chatbots allow you to communicate with Enni in the most personal style. Rather than be limited to interacting with an app through the designed interface you can directly interact with Enni and ask her the questions that you want to know. Enni is available any time and from any platform. Are you on a laptop? A mobile, tablet, Android or Apple device? No worries, speak to Enni through any of these devices. You’re not limited to only interacting with Enni through the phone you downloaded the application on as you would be with an app. Have you ever spent ages searching through your phone for an app you know is there but can’t find? Do you get frustrated by having to download updates for apps – or worse not having enough storage space on your phone to download it? Enni living on Facebook Messenger resolves this problem. Enni is smart. Using the latest advances in natural language processing, interact with Enni like you would a friend. AI is transforming the way we do life, interacting directly with Enni allows you to utilise AI to manage your food in the most effective way: cutting food waste, saving you money and the environment.
