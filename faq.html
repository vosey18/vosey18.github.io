AI is transforming the way we do life, interacting directly with Enni allows you to utilise AI to manage your food in the most effective way: cutting food waste, saving you money and the environment.

How big of a problem is food waste?

Food waste is a massive problem in the UK and World-wide.
Each year in the UK:
* 4.4m tonnes of avoidable food is wasted
* This is £13 billion thrown away
* Through the production of this food, 19m tonnes of greenhouse gasses are released into the atmosphere
* Eliminating this food waste would have the same effect as taking 25% of cars of the road in the UK
* 70% of consumers don’t check what they have at home before going shopping
* Throwing out 1kg of beef has the same effect as wasting 50,000 litres of water
* Produced but uneaten food occupies almost 1.4 billion hectares of land; which is nearly 30% of the world’s agricultural land area.
