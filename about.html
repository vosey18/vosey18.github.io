Who is Enni?


Enni is your intelligent AI companion for managing your food. We’re giving you the tools to simplify your sustainability goals.

70% of people don’t check their fridge before going shopping.

Do you really need to buy another onion or some more beef? Do you have ‘Enni’ left at home? – Ask Enni.

How much chicken do you need to cook that roast dinner on Sunday? – Ask Enni.





Enni is a social enterprise started by students from the University of Edinburgh. The idea was formed after the three co-founders shared a flat for 2 years, and during this time unintentionally wasted loads of food.
